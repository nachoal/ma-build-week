# Five-person cold-viewer test

Status: protocol ready; external participants and results pending.

## Prompt

Show the frozen under-three-minute video once. Give no product explanation
beforehand. Within ten seconds of the closing frame ask:

> What changed for the learner, and what does MA do when Japanese becomes too
> fast to understand?

Then ask:

> Was any part a replay or demonstration rather than a live learner action?

## Pass rule

At least 4/5 viewers independently communicate both ideas:

1. a zero beginner learned and retried the same useful restaurant obligation;
2. MA turns a breakdown into a small controlled repair lesson and resumes.

Any viewer who believes the labeled replay was live, or believes MA grades
pronunciation, identifies a comprehension blocker even if their first answer
otherwise passes.
